Menu Simon Garrett

I grew up in England where I have a degree in Music with Visual Practice from Brighton University and I like to combine the two media whenever I get the chance. I can shoot video, create animations, add post-production graphics or come up with unique treatments for promotion with or without original sound design and music.

I have strong skills in all the relevant Adobe software for static and moving visuals in addition to many years of practical camerawork, rotoscoping, traditional animation and I've recently discovered animating with Processing.

I play guitar, bass and drums, record with Logic Pro and I use Ableton for DJ'ing, Arranging and live Midi control. I like heavy rhythm but have a great ear for melody too. I'm more of a funky backbeat player than a jazz guy.
